POPULAR QUESTIONS

How would you gather Attendees data?

All Business Summit continuously gathers the data through online registration portals who have confirmed in attending this year which is near to the event, Hence we offer coupons to the Attendees, So when they register through our site they get Discounts on base price and this is how they permit to access their data information ( Opt-In) and also through Email Survey who showed their interest for attending current year.

Is there are any refund / replacement policies?

We strictly don't have refund but we have replacement policy, before we starting the project we will make a contract with the client regarding the replacement, Guarantee level and other information etc. so if any data goes wrong in the quarantine what we mention in the contract. We will do 1+1 replacement from the contractual period of 30 days with no additional cost.

Will I get updated contacts prior to the event?

We will keep refreshing the list every 10 days As soon as we received the contacts from Registration Portal we will send them for verification process then we will add it to our master database and you will be receiving the updated contacts once we done with our re-verification process. You don’t need to pay anything for the updated contacts as it will be included on this purchase.
