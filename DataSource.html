Data Source

Global Expo Data tracking tool

Global Expo Data lists are built by our attendee intelligence tracking tool, which monitors Trade Publication Subscribers, Professional Journal Subscribers, Conference Attendee registrations, Social Media Groups and Hashtags around events and conferences globally.

We collect basic data from our attendee intelligence tracking tool and sources, match it with our master file (We own a database of 29 million B2B opt-in contacts) and append the necessary contact details which is done manually by our 300+ data research.
